About the Great Ainz Ooal Gown

Ainz Ooal Gown , formerly known as Momonga the Guildmaster

is the main protagonist of the Overlord series. He is the guildmaster of Ainz Ooal Gown, Overlord of the Great Tomb of Nazarick, and the creator of Pandora's Actor. He is regarded as the highest of the Almighty Forty-One Supreme Beings by the NPCs of Nazarick.


In the New World

He is the Sorcerer King of the Sorcerer Kingdom and the most powerful magic caster in the world. His other identity is famously known as "Momon," a dark warrior and leader of Darkness, an adamantite ranked adventurer group of that nation. He is an adamantite class adventurer and the strongest adventurer known in E-Rantel.


Personality

While Ainz is a cautious and stingy person, he will always try to plan ahead and control details beforehand in order to avoid any haphazard actions that can endanger Nazarick's existence. This means going so far as lying to the inhabitants in the New World that he can't use one of his Super-tier spells for years to come after using it once. Since being transferred to the New World and becoming an actual undead being, Ainz has felt himself becoming very cold and calculating while his emotions are repressed. He was not the least bit afraid of lying to or provoking his combatants, using each and every trick he can think of to overcome the odds. He takes satisfaction from experimenting with his powers by measuring the extent of it to the point where he heartlessly brainwashes and kills people. Everything Ainz aims to do was to better himself, rack up accomplishments, and master new ways to use his magic and stats in the New World.


Appearance

As an Overlord, Ainz Ooal Gown is a skeleton with a robe and staff. He is a lich, an undead creature with a skeletal body, and his face is a bare skull. He wears a set of elaborate robes and carries a staff. His appearance is that of a stereotypical dark wizard. He is a tall, skeletal figure who is dressed in a black robe decorated with gold trimmings. He has a pair of red points of light in the eye sockets of his skull, which are not actually eyes. His voice is described as being hollow and echoing, as if it is coming from an empty space.


Abilities

As an Overlord, Ainz Ooal Gown is a powerful magic caster with a wide range of abilities. He is capable of casting a variety of spells, including offensive, defensive, and support spells. He is also able to summon and control undead creatures, as well as create magical constructs. In addition to his magical abilities, Ainz is also a skilled warrior, capable of using a variety of weapons and fighting styles. He is also able to use his magic to enhance his physical abilities, making him a formidable opponent in combat. Ainz is also able to use his magic to manipulate time and space, allowing him to teleport and create portals to other locations. He is also able to create illusions and manipulate the minds of others, making him a master of deception and manipulation.
